The Brief & Context
At the first session of Studio 1 our group received an assignment to define a topics that we individually research to have better understanding how different factors can impact humans and other species’ life.
As I was raised by a teacher mother I was always surrounded by teaching and the difficulties of it. As an adult, I started to have more discussions with her on the rising difficulites of the profession of hers and others (ie. friends of her, teachers in my network...etc.)
Fact / Validity Check
The National Education Association ran a research in 2024, discovering the well-being / difficulties teachers face daily.
The survey concluded that stress and burnout is the top consering factor, and it is heavily impacted by student/discipline. In 2025, the University of Missouri surveyed teachers if they plan to leave their current job. The research showed due to lack of support, workload and challenging students make them leave their profession. The Education Week magazine introduced the Teacher-Morale-Index to see the state of teaching in the US. Their latest index concluded that on a -100 to +100 scale the current TMI is at -13. The OECD has framework for well-being of teachers.
