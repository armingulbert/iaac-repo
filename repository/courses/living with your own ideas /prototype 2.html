Sleep-O-Meter — Prototype #2

Goal: explore a prosthetic concept that lets a user modulate biorhythm and heartbeat to influence sleep demand and efficiency.

Sleep like a bird
12 sec micro-nap
Sleep like a bear
Month-long deep sleep

Idea

The brief asked for a prosthetic solution that helps the potential user “become something else.” Animals display very different sleep patterns—birds require little sleep while some species hibernate for months. The concept explores dialing between those modes by adjusting biorhythm and heartbeat.

People’s sleep demand also fluctuates in short and long phases. The Sleep-O-Meter imagines a prosthetic that helps modulate those phases for well-being, lower stress, and potentially longer lifespan.

Prototype

The current prototype is low-fidelity and demonstrates the physical appearance and interaction. Testers can rotate the top wheel (a simple “potmeter-type” control) to simulate adjustment. Other elements are visual only.
