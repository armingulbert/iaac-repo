Proto #2
Sleep-O-Meter
The following page covers a prototype creation of a prosthetics heartbeat and biorhythm meter created for an ideation brief received during our “Living with your ideas” course. The purpose of this prototype is to showcase how we can extend the limitations of the human body.
Idea

The received brief highlighted that MDEF students must deliver a prosthetic solution that would help the potential user become something else.

Two major drivers shaped the Sleep-O-Meter idea: (1) adjusting biorhythm/heartbeat to influence sleep demand and efficiency (some animals sleep very little, others hibernate long), and (2) humans’ sleep demand often changes for short or long periods. Combining these, we imagine a prosthetic that lets users tune body needs, potentially improving life quality and longevity.

Prototype

The current prototype is a low-fidelity artefact to visualise the Sleep-O-Meter’s physical appearance and to provoke discussion around potential usage and user experience. Functionally it offers a simple “potmeter” experience: testers rotate the top white wheel to mimic an actual meter adjustment; the remaining elements are static and serve visualisation purposes only.
