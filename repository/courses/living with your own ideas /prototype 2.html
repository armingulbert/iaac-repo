Sleep-O-Meter — Prototype #2

Goal:The following page covers a prototype creation of a prostethics heartbeat and biorythm meter created for an ideation brief recieved during our “Living with your ideas” course. The purpose of this prototype to showcase how we can extend the limitations of the humand body.

Sleep like a bird

Sleep like a bear
Month-long deep sleep

Idea

The receive brief highlighted that MDEF students must deliver a prosthetic solution that would help the potential user become something else. There are two major trigger / driver behind the idea of creating a Sleep-O-Meter that helps the user to adjust his/her/their biorythm and heartbeat to influence the sleep demand and efficiency. Based on the lecture of the course I learnt that we can adjust ourselves and bodies becoming an animal. Animals can show different sleep demand and efficiency - like birds require the least amount of sleep, in the meantime certain spieces take long, even quarter-year-long winter sleep. The second driver of the idea is a common situation happens us, humans, that our sleep demand can change eventually for short and long-term. This phase can be a couple of days, but even years length. The combination of the two drivers resulted the idea that us, humans, we can enable ourselves to modify our body and its needs with a prosthetics. The benefit of the artefact would be better life condition, less stress and potentially longer lifespan.

Prototype

The current prototype is a low-fidelity one, and its purpose to visualise the Sleep-O-Meter physical appearance, initiate discussions on potential use and the related experience of users. In terms of functionality the current prototype cover a simple “potmeter-type” of experience, testers can rotate the top-white-wheel replicating the experience of an actual meter-adjustment. Rest of the elemts are not functioning, they serve visualisation purposes only.
